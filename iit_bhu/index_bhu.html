The Gap

Doctors can't hear what language whispers

Mental health crises don't arrive suddenly — they emerge slowly through subtle shifts in how patients speak and write. Hope fades, isolation grows, sleep disappears from sentences. Current systems hear none of it.

🕐

Reactive, Not Proactive

Clinicians only intervene after severe symptoms manifest. MindGuard flags risk 2–6 weeks earlier using language biomarkers.

📋

Unstructured Data Ignored

Therapy notes, intake forms, and chat logs sit unused. Our NLP pipeline extracts actionable signal from all of it.

🌐

India's Language Barrier

Patients express distress in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu. English-only models miss 70% of India's population entirely.

📊

No Unified Clinician View

Even when signals exist, doctors have no risk dashboard. MindGuard delivers a scored, prioritized patient queue.
